Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the demo project Videoflix, a non-commercial, experimental software developed for educational and demonstration purposes. The platform allows user registration and login, but data is only processed for testing purposes and is not used in a live environment.

1. General Information

Videoflix is a demo platform designed for educational and testing purposes. It allows users to create accounts, log in, and interact with the platform in a simulated environment. However, no real services or content are provided, and no personal data is shared with third parties.

2. Data Collection

When you register on the Videoflix platform, we collect basic information, such as:

  • Username

  • Password (stored in a securely hashed format)

This information is used solely for the purpose of allowing users to log in and interact with the demo environment. No additional data is collected unless explicitly stated during testing.

5. Use of Cookies

Videoflix may use local cookies or session data to maintain user sessions and preferences during testing. These cookies are not used for tracking purposes and are only stored locally for the duration of your session.

6. Third-Party Services

Videoflix does not integrate with any third-party services, such as analytics, advertising, or social media platforms. All data processing is done within the demo environment, and no personal information is shared with external providers.

Since this is a demo project, any user data collected during testing will be retained only for the duration of the project and may be deleted at any time. As this is not a live service, there is no long-term data retention policy.
